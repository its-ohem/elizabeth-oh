WRITING REPORTS & PROPOSALS

Writing Reports & Proposals focused on the fundamentals and best practices of grant writing, including the skills to seek, solicit, and receive grant awards from foundations and local/state/federal government sources. Through this class, I collaborated with classmates to build an effective and compelling proposal to the Greater Cincinnati Foundation to fund afterschool education programs at the Winton Place Youth Center. I learned to apply persuasive writing strategies to real, tangible proposals and draft budgets, timelines, and guides for non-profit organizations.

"DEI GRANT WRITING"

The “DEI Grant Writing” presentation is a brief, educational slideshow that summarizes the history of DEI and the effects of federal censorship beginning in 2024. By gathering relevant academic articles and recent news trends, I illustrated the gravity of DEI to the practice of grant writing using data visualization and direct quotes.

This presentation allowed me to integrate the persuasive and research-driven skills developed in this class by connecting broad sociopolitical contexts to effective grant writing practices. In this project, I synthesized academic research and recent developments to explain the historical significance DEI and implications of federal censorship, demonstrating my ability to situate professional writing within evolving public discourse. By designing a concise, visually engaging slideshow that incorporated data visualization and strategic use of direct quotes, I applied principles of clarity, persuasion, and audience awareness to communicate complex issues to a general audience. Overall, this project highlights my capacity to connect classroom learning with real-world advocacy and reinforces my preparedness for professional writing and communication roles.
